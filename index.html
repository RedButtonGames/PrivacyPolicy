User has the right to request that his data be deleted from our servers. To do this, follow the instructions below:

  3. Click the Removed sub-tab and click on “Battle of Titans” app.
  4. Click “Send Request” to trigger request Battle of Titans delete info it has about you from Facebook and our servers.
